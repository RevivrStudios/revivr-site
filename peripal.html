PeriPal

Your personal health intelligence companion.

Welcome to Your Health Journey

PeriPal is your personal health intelligence companion, designed to help you navigate perimenopause with confidence and clarity. Track, understand, and manage your health journey with an app built specifically for your needs.

Track What Matters to You

Choose from multiple health categories including sleep, mood, energy, period tracking, weight, stress, libido, and skin health. PeriPal prioritizes what you want to improve and makes daily check-ins effortless.

Automate Your Insights

Connect with Apple Health to automatically track your sleep quality and daily steps. Less manual entry, more magic. PeriPal works seamlessly with your existing health data to provide comprehensive insights.

Personalized Health Tracking

Track perimenopause symptoms with intuitive emoji-based sliders. Rate the intensity of hot flashes, mood changes, and other symptoms quickly and easily. Your data helps identify patterns and triggers.

Build Your Complete Health Profile

Answer personalized questions to build a comprehensive health profile. PeriPal learns about your unique experience and provides tailored insights and recommendations based on your specific symptoms and patterns.

Intelligent Insights

PeriPal uses advanced analytics to identify patterns in your health data, helping you understand what affects your symptoms and well-being. Get actionable insights that empower you to make informed decisions about your health.

Privacy & Security

Your health data is deeply personal. PeriPal keeps all your information secure and private, with optional Apple Health integration for seamless tracking. You're always in control of your data.

Built with Empathy

Created by someone who understands the challenges of perimenopause firsthand, PeriPal is designed to support you through this transition with compassion, clarity, and evidence-based insights.
