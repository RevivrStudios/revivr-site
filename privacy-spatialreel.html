Privacy Policy for Spatial Reel

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Your information when You use the Spatial Reel app.

Zero Data Collection Policy

Spatial Reel is designed with absolute privacy in mind.

  • No Accounts or Sign-ins: The application does not require or allow you to create an account.
  • No Network Calls: The application does not communicate with any external servers. There is no backend infrastructure.
  • No Analytics: We do not track how you use the app, which reels you create, or how often you view your memories.
  • Local Data Only: Everything — your reels, your photos, and your preferences — stays entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded or analyzed externally. Deleting a reel removes its photos from the app's local storage immediately.
