Spatial Tree

Bring festive 3D Christmas trees to your physical space.

The Experience

Bring Magic Home

Spatial Tree is a native visionOS application that brings festive cheer to your spatial environment. Place, customize, and share beautiful 3D Christmas trees that feel naturally anchored in your physical space.

Realistic Spatial Placement

Using advanced ARKit plane detection, Spatial Tree lets you place realistic 3D trees exactly where you want them. Watch as the magic unfolds as you plant your tree on any flat surface in your room.

A Gallery of Festive Styles

Choose from a curated collection of unique tree styles. From the traditional "The Standard" to more whimsical designs like "Ginger-Snap", "Crimson Coil", and the elegant "Bullion Stack", there's a tree for every mood.

Intuitive Interactive Gestures

Master the magic with your hands. Use your left hand to freely translate (move) the tree, and your right hand to scale and rotate with intuitive pinch gestures. The interaction feels invisible and natural.

Share the Joy with SharePlay

Decorate together! With SharePlay integration, you can use FaceTime to synchronize tree selection and customization with friends and family, making holiday preparation a shared spatial experience.

Complete Customization

Fine-tune your festive atmosphere. Adjust the tree size dynamically, control the snowfall height and intensity to create a winter wonderland indoors, and toggle festive background music to complete the mood.

Built for Vision Pro

Designed specifically for visionOS, Spatial Tree utilizes world-sensing and hand-tracking to create a truly immersive experience that honors the space you live in. Whether you're decorating a small apartment or a large living area, your tree belongs there.
