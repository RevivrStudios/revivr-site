VisionMark

Designed for Apple Vision Pro

The essential tool for inspecting and annotating images on visionOS.

Simply drag and drop an image or select one from your library, then use a full suite of markup tools to highlight details, add notes, or draw freely. Supports trackpad, stylus, and direct touch with complete privacy.

TeleVisionPrompter

Designed for Apple Vision Pro

The ultimate teleprompter experience for spatial computing.

Never memorize a script again. TeleVisionPrompter floats your script in your real-world view, moving with you or staying anchored to a point in space.

Perfect for content creators, public speakers, and professionals conducting meetings in VR. Features include customizable text settings, playback controls, virtual distance adjustment, and recording capabilities.

Spatial Tree

Designed for Apple Vision Pro

Bring festive 3D Christmas trees to your physical space.

Spatial Tree is a native visionOS application that brings festive cheer to your spatial environment. Place, customize, and share beautiful 3D Christmas trees that feel naturally anchored in your physical space.

Features include realistic spatial placement, a curated gallery of unique tree styles, intuitive hand gesture controls, and SharePlay support to decorate together with friends and family.

PeriPal

Designed for iPhone, iPad

Your personal health intelligence companion.

PeriPal helps you navigate perimenopause with confidence. Track sleep, mood, energy, symptoms, and more with an intuitive app designed specifically for your health journey.

Connect with Apple Health to automatically track sleep quality and daily steps. Get personalized insights based on your unique patterns and symptoms. Built with empathy by someone who understands.
