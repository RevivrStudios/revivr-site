Spatial Reel

Your memories, in the round.

Designed for Apple Vision Pro.

Coming Soon

A physical, tactile memory experience.

Spatial Reel reimagines the classic picture reel toy as a spatial computing experience. Instead of clicking through a flat grid, you grab a 3D viewer that floats in your room, pull it up to your face, and your photos appear around you. Built natively for mixed reality, it lets you hold your memories in your hands.

Grab It, Bring It, Look

Not a UI panel, but a hand-tracked, physical 3D object. Bring the viewer closer, and the room gracefully dims as your photos take center stage.

True Spatial Depth

Spatial Reel automatically detects genuine Apple Spatial Photos and renders them in true stereo depth alongside your regular photos.

Curated Reels

Photos are organized into 7-photo collections echoing the classic cardboard reels, making memories fast and focused to browse.

Tactile Dial Navigation

Turn the brushed-gold dial with a twist/drag gesture. Feel the satisfying animated snap as you advance to the next memory.

Local On-Device Storage

Everything stays on your device. Your reels and memories are stored locally, with no accounts, no cloud uploads, and complete privacy.

Making memories feel physical again.
