VisionMark

Professional watermarking for your Vision Pro spatial captures.

The App

Load Your Spatial Captures

VisionMark welcomes you with a clean, intuitive interface designed specifically for Vision Pro. Simply tap "Load Image from Photos" to begin watermarking your spatial captures.

Powerful Adjustment Tools

Fine-tune your watermark with precision controls. Rotate left or right, adjust positioning, and customize every aspect of your watermark—all through an eye-tracking interface that feels natural in spatial computing.

Choose Your Style

Select from a variety of watermark styles and colors. Whether you prefer subtle or bold, VisionMark gives you the creative control to match your brand and aesthetic.

Quick Actions

Save your watermarked image, copy it to clipboard, assign to a contact, print, or save to Files—all with simple, accessible actions. Your workflow stays smooth and efficient.

Advanced Features

Access advanced features and settings to customize VisionMark to your exact needs. From batch processing to custom watermark templates, VisionMark grows with your creative workflow.

Privacy First

All image processing happens locally on your Vision Pro. Your photos never leave your device, and VisionMark requires no internet connection to function. Your creative work stays yours.

Built for Creators

Whether you're a spatial photographer, content creator, or just want to protect your Vision Pro memories, VisionMark makes it effortless to add professional watermarks to your spatial captures.
